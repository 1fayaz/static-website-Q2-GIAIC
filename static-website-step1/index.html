Nutrition

Eating a balanced diet is essential for your overall health. Here's a quick guide to nutrition:

  1. Carbohydrates: Provide energy for your body.
  2. Proteins: Essential for building and repairing muscles.
  3. Fats: Help with brain function and cell repair.
Health Tips

To maintain good health, consider these daily habits:

  • Drink plenty of water.
  • Eat colorful fruits and vegetables.
  • Exercise regularly for at least 30 minutes.
  • Get 7-9 hours of sleep each night.
